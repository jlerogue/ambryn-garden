Primarily found in the [[2.Campaigns/Stonephalanx Island Campaign/Locations/Mistwood Coil\|Mistwood Coil]] of [[Locations/Regions/Banzi Hilands/Stonephalanx Islands\|Stonephalanx Islands]].
