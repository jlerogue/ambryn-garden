### Requirements

Must abide by the **Core Commandments** of Revantum Nova. These were created communally upon the City's founding.

**Type**

Civic, Political

**Form of Address**

Lord Mayor

**Source of Authority**

Plate Mayors

**Length of Term**

20 years.
