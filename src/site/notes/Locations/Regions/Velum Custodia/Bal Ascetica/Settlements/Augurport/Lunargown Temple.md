A place of peace that appears in a variety of locations, but is only ever reliably found in [[Locations/Regions/Velum Custodia/Bal Ascetica/Settlements/Augurport/Augurport\|Augurport]].
- A ziggurat that manifests in the light of the full moon.
- A large, empty square toward the back of Augurport is kept bare to occupy the temple.
- Medicinal flowers called [[1.Ambryn/Flora/Pallid Flutes\|Pallid Flutes]] which lose their efficacy as soon as they are removed from the temple.
- The temple only appears in places where leylines intersect.
- Many people fall in love in the temple.
- The temple was created by the [[1.Ambryn/Subdeities, Eldritch Beings, Legendary Figures, and Creatures of Renown/Penumbral Witch\|Penumbral Witch]].
